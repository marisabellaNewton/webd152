Our Story at Pacific Trails

Our Mission

Experience serenity and adventure in perfect harmony. Pacific Trails Resort offers an unforgettable escape where you can reconnect with nature while enjoying modern comforts.

Our History

Since its founding in 1996 by Robert and Isabella Vandemberg, Pacific Trails Resort has been a cherished getaway for those seeking a perfect balance of adventure and relaxation.

Come Visit Us

Discover the magic of Pacific Trails Resort, where the scent of pine meets the salty breeze of the Pacific. Explore our guided nature trails, enjoy local cuisine, or unwind by the pool. Whether you'er seeking an invigorating adventure or a quiet escape, we offer something for everyone.

Take in breathtaking views of the Pacific as you hike along scenic coastal trails, or relax with a rejuvenating spa treatment inspired by the natural surroundings. Join us for unforgettable momments that bring balance and serenity to your life.

Our Values

Our core values include fostering a deep connection with nature, promoting sustainable practices, and creating memorable experiences for all guests. We are committed to environmental stewardship and community engagement, ensuring that Pacific Trails Resort remains a haven of tranquility.
