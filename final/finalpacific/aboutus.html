Our Story at Pacific Trails

Our Mission

Experience serenity and adventure in perfect harmony. Pacific Trails Resort offers an unforgettable escape where you can reconnect with nature while enjoying modern comforts.

Our History

Since its founding in 1996 by Robert and Isabella Vandemberg, Pacific Trails Resort has been a cherished getaway for those seeking a perfect balance of adventure and relaxation. Nestled along the breathtaking California coastline, our resort combines the beauty of the outdoors with the luxury of fine amenities.

Come Visit Us

Discover the magic of Pacific Trails Resort, where the scent of pine meets the salty breeze of the Pacific. Explore our guided nature trails, enjoy local cuisine, or unwind by the pool. Whatever your heart desires, we have something for everyone.

Our Values

Nature Connection, Community Bonding, Adventure, Relaxation & Comfort
